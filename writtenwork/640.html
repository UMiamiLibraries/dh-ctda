Pelusín y los pájaros

Play script (Spanish)

Notes:

A version of Pelusín y los pájaros was televised on Tele-Teatro Sabado on June 24, 1961 until. This version, also known as "Aventuras de Pelusín del Monte," was soon condensed to a half an hour televised show that appeared every Saturday from July 22, 1961 until the latter half of 1963.
