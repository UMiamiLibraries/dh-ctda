Miami-Dade County Auditorium

Also known as: Dade County Auditorium

Notes:

This has been Greater Miami's premier performing arts center for grand opera, symphony, theater, concerts, ballets, lectures and other programs of community interest. It is also the center of Hispanic cultural activity since it spoke Spanish for the first time in 1964.
