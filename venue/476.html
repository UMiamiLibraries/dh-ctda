Café Teatro Kimbara Cumbara

Notes:

1644 SW 8th Street
Miami, Florida 33135-5220
Telephone: (305) 642-8822

Kimbara Cumbara is a space for different art forms such as music, theater, visual arts, and photography. Its goal is to highlight Little Havana and 8th Street as an alternative cultural scene. Fabio Díaz-Vilela Enterprises produces and directs its artistic programming.
