Teatro 8

Hispanic Theater Guild bought this space in 1999. It used to be Teatro Zayón and, originally Sala-Teatro Casanova, bought and built by María Julia Casanova. The artistic director of Teatro 8 was Marcos Casanova (no relation to María Julia) until 2012. 

In July 2012, La Sociedad Actoral Hispanoamericana assumed the rental. The Puerto Rican Miguel Sahid became artistic director, and Colombian Miguel Mejía was its producer.
