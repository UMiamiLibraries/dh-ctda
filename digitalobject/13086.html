Costume breakdown for the ballet, "¡Sí señor! ¡Es mi son!"


This document lists costumes for “¡Si Señor! ¡Es Mi Son!”. The fourth page contains Costume Expense Projections for the play.

Notes:

This document lists costumes for “¡Si Señor! ¡Es Mi Son!”. The fourth page contains Costume Expense Projections for the play. These were Randy Barceló's last designs before he passed away on December 6, 1994.
