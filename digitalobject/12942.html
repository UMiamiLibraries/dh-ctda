Program for the production, "Los Fantasmas de Tulemón"


The front cover of the playbill has images of 4 of the play's characters. It also mentions that this particular production was performed in 1999 perhaps during a festival, "Fiesta!" Inside, the playbill gives a list of the cast, artistic director and producer, set/stage directors and designers. Also it provides a short summary of the play, author, company, and sponsors, and of each of the cast members and the set desingers, including Leandro Soto. It provides a background of Leandro Soto's life. Finally, it includes some advertisements.
