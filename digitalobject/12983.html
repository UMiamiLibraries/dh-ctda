Graciela Más (La monja) in the production, "La estrella y la monja"


In this photograph, the Monja (Graciela Mas) is at the center, holding a candle in each hand and she has a rosary hanging off of her right wrist. She is surrounded by many locos, representing the male gaze of the media frenzy, who are grabbing her and trying to seduce her.
