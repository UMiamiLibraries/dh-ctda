Cap-a-Pie

Premiere: World

Directing team: +

Name Function(s)
María Irene Fornés Director

Notes: +

This is a bilingual piece based on the memories, fantasies, and aspirations of eight young actors who come from different parts of Latin America and the Caribbean. "For a breezy relaxation, and for theatre that contains something as opposed to artfully displayed nothing, I had a better, and friefer time, at Maria Irene Fornes's "CAP-A-Pie" (The Village Voice, May 19, 1975).
