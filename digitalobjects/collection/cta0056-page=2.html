Martha del Río Digital Collection

Notes: +

    This collection includes photographs, theater programs, and newspaper clippings belonging to Cuban actress Martha del Río. The physical materials were digitized by Daniel Correa-Manzor at the University of Miami Richter Libraries. Metadata was created by Lilianne Lugo. The physical materials remain with the artist. The digital collection was revised and published by Lillian Manzor (2017).
