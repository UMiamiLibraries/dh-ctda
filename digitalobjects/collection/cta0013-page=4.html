Teatro de las Estaciones Digital Collection

Notes: +

    The Teatro de las Estaciones Digital Collection documents the work of this theater group from Matanzas, Cuba. The collection primarily includes videos and photographs donated by Rubén Darío Salazar and Norge Espinosa as well as reviews and e-blasts. Other videos of Teatro de las Estaciones are available at CTDA Productions Collection.
