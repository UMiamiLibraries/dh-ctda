Maikel Sánchez Digital Collection

Notes: +

    This collection includes materials belonging to Cuban designer, Maikel Sánchez. You can find many programs from the Teatro Musical de La Habana. The physical materials were digitized by Daniel Correa-Manzor at the University of Miami Libraries. Metadata was created by Gabriel Lama and Melissa Hurtado. The physical materials remain with the artist. The digital collection was revised and published by Lillian Manzor (2017).
